Tini Rapids Adventure

The Adasewase Canopy Walk in Ghana is a breathtaking experience suspended 30 meters above the forest floor in Adasewase forest. Spanning seven bridges, it offers panoramic views of the rainforest and a close encounter with nature. It’s a must-visit for adventure seekers and eco-tourism lovers.!

Family Float Tour

A peaceful rafting experience perfect for families and beginners. Enjoy the scenic views and wildlife along the riverbanks.The Umbrella Rock in Ghana is a stunning natural formation at the middle forest of Adasewase in the Atiwa East District, Eastern Region. A massive flat rock perched on a slim base, it resembles an umbrella and can shelter 12–15 people.

Sunset Scenic Cruise

Unwind with a calm river cruise as the sun sets over the Adasewase Forest. A perfect end to your day.
